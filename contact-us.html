Facilities

ProChemTech maintains a main office and laboratory in Brockway, PA. Plant #1, also at our 10 acre Brockway site, manufactures our full line of chemical products and all equipment. Plant #2, which manufactures a full line of industrial water treatment chemicals, is located, along with our Western Regional office, in the North Pinal Industrial Park, Apache Junction, AZ.

Sales and Marketing

Service/sales representatives are stationed in the Main Office, Western Regional Office, and throughout the United States. The company is represented in Mexico by Ararat in Mexico City, in the Philippines by Alpine Systems Corporation and in Australia by Water Treatment Services, Ltd. Due to our status of being the sole manufacturer of various glass surface coatings, some specialty glass chemical products have been exported to Mexico, Puerto Rico, Italy, England, Guatemala, France, Argentina and Columbia.
